Why us?

O'Donnell Park Barging was set up in 1996 by the O'Donnell and Parkes families of Marlborough. We have been established within Marlborough for the past 19 years and we hold Marlborough close in our hearts. Our founding families roots run deep in Marlborough and have done so for many generations.
We have combined skipper experience of over 60 years, and staff, vessels, and equipment capable of undertaking most if not all on water tasks necessary.

Meet the team

Our services

We are in the business of on water logistics. Being able to transport a customer's product to a location, or provide a customer a specific service in a safe, timely and cost effective manner. We offer a wide range of services, from crane work to livestock transportation.
We want to make life easy for our customers no job is too big or too small.
Call today for a quote or if you have any queries regarding moving your cargo.

Our equipment

Our vessel fleet currently consists of two motorised vessels, the MV Lana and the MV Ngapuhi and Onewaka our dumb-barge.
Our vessels are well equipped and our staff knowledgeable in all areas where we operate. Delivery and collection, mooring installation and vehicle transportation are just the tip of the iceberg regarding our capabilites.
